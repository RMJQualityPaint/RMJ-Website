Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

How long does it take to get an estimate?

We typically respond within 24 business hours — often same-day. For urgent projects, call us directly at (706) 473-1908 for the fastest response.

Is the estimate really free?

Yes, 100%. We come out, assess the project, and give you a clear written quote at no cost and with zero obligation to hire us.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Central Georgia including Lake Oconee, Eatonton, Milledgeville, Reynolds, Forsyth, Madison, Monticello, Gray, Greensboro, Macon, and surrounding areas.

Do you work with property management companies?

Absolutely. We have dedicated partnerships with property managers across the region. Volume pricing, priority scheduling, and detailed documentation are all standard for PM accounts.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. RMJ Quality Painting is fully licensed and carries comprehensive liability insurance. We can provide a certificate of insurance upon request.

What information should I have ready when I call?

Just a general idea of the project scope (what rooms/surfaces, approximate size, any paint preferences) and your timeline. We handle the rest during the estimate visit.
